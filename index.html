About

“For I dipped into the future, far as human eye could see, Saw the Vision of the world, and all the wonder that would be.” — Alfred Lord Tennyson

I believe in the beauty and the power of the ideas that shape our future. I believe that we have the fundamental responsibility to push humanity beyond its limits, into a world that, as the Baron Tennyson describes, far surpasses the wonders of imagination.

My work focuses on exploring and building the technologies of the future. I write about how technological advancement intersects with social structures, economic systems, and sustainability. I'm particularly interested in post-labour economics and the journey humanity must trek to reach that point.

Here are some areas I focus on:

  • Philosophy & Ethics
  • Robotics & AI
  • Post-Labour Economics
  • Sustainability
  • Creative Destruction
  • Culture & Religion
